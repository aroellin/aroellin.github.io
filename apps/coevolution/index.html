Concordant pairs gain an edge at rate ρ sc,0 and lose it at rate ρ sc,1; discordant pairs gain at ρ sd,0 and lose at ρ sd,1. Each discordant edge triggers an opinion flip at rate 2η. The β-presets set η = β and ρ = n. Edits apply immediately, even to a stopped run; Restart re-initialises the graph.
